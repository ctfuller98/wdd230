Our Target Audience

The Chamber's target audience is the businesses in the county and the people who live there. The Chamber stands to be the "home guard" of the free enterprise system. Our local projects include community service events, visitor development, public relations, retail promotions, economic development, legislation action, agribusiness and other projects working for solutions to community needs and problems.

Business Owners

Business Owners own prosperity depends upon the development of a prosperous, healthy and happy community. The Chamber helps business owners make connections and grow.

  • Scenario 1- A new business owner who is trying to make connections and get the word out.
  • Scenario 2- A long time business owner looking to find out about local seminars and workshops in the area.
  • Scenario 3- A resource for business owners to know how new laws might affect their business.

Community Members

Community Members can visit this site to find information about their community and neighborhood, upcoming events, local business, and other things.

  • Scenario 1- A family who has recently moved into the area is able to find professionals they are needing to do repairs to the home they just purchased.
  • Scenario 2- A mom looking for something to do as a family is able to find an upcoming community event to go to for the weekend.
